Kansas

"Ad Astra Per Aspera"

Often referred to as the 'Sunflower State,' Kansas boasts vast expanses of golden prairies and picturesque landscapes. From its rich agricultural heritage to its pivotal role in American history, Kansas offers a tapestry of culture and tradition waiting to be explored.

Facts about Kansas

  • United States' top producer of wheat
  • In the heart of Tornado Alley
  • Geographic center of the US
  • Home of Dorothy in the Wizard of Oz
